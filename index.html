How to Play Scrabble

Place letter tiles on the board to form valid English words. Your first tile must be placed on the center square. Score points based on the letters used and special bonus squares.

  • Validate Word: Checks your placed tiles, confirms if they form valid words, and updates your score.
  • New Game: Resets the board, tiles, and your score to start a new match.
  • Refresh Tiles: Click this icon to discard your current rack and draw a new set of tiles (if available).
  • Garbage Bin: Drag unwanted tiles from your rack into the garbage bin to discard them and draw replacements.

Use letter tiles from your rack, form words on the board, and aim for the highest score possible!
